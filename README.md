# Nexoras AI

Nexoras AI is an AI-powered educational platform designed to help students learn smarter and prepare for their future careers.

## Features

- AI Study Planner
- Resume Builder
- AI Mock Interviews
- Career Roadmaps
- Productivity Dashboard
- JEE Preparation
- NEET Preparation
- UPSC Preparation
- GATE Preparation
- CAT Preparation
- SAT, GRE, IELTS & TOEFL Support

## Website

https://nexoras.online

## Mission

To make high-quality educational tools accessible to students through AI-powered learning and career guidance.
